# PART 1: WHAT IS AN NDE? / 第一部分：什么是濒死体验？ (3 minutes)

## Slide: "什么是濒死体验？" / "What is a Near-Death Experience?"

**English:**
"A near-death experience—or NDE—is a profound psychological event that occurs when someone faces death or a life-threatening crisis. People describe a range of sensations during these moments."

**中文:**
"濒死体验——或简称NDE——是指当某人面临死亡或生命危机时所经历的深刻心理事件。人们在这些时刻描述了一系列感觉。"

**English:**
"Common features include out-of-body experiences—feeling yourself floating outside your body, viewing the scene from above. Many report tunnels and light—moving through passages toward intense, peaceful light. Others describe life reviews—watching their entire life flash before them in moments. And almost all report a sense of spiritual awareness—feeling connected to something greater than themselves."

**中文:**
"常见特征包括灵魂出窍体验——感到自己漂浮在身体之外，从上方观看场景。许多人报告看到隧道和光——穿过通道走向强烈的、平静的光。其他人描述人生回顾——看到他们整个人生在瞬间闪过。几乎所有人都报告有灵性觉知——感到与某种比自己更伟大的东西相连。"

**English:**
"Now here's the key question: is this a real phenomenon? The answer is yes—approximately 4-15% of the global population reports having had an NDE. But the research on this phenomenon—especially in Asian populations—remains limited. That's where our study comes in."

**中文:**
"现在，这里是关键问题：这是真实现象吗？答案是肯定的——全球约4-15%的人口报告过濒死体验。但对这一现象的研究——尤其是在亚洲人群中——仍然有限。这正是我们研究的意义所在。"

# PART 2: RESEARCH DEVELOPMENT / 第二部分：濒死体验研究的发展 (3 minutes)

## Slide: "濒死体验研究的发展" / "The Development of NDE Research"

**English:**
"Let's look at how NDE research has evolved. In 1892, Albert Heim conducted pioneering work with mountaineers who'd experienced accidents. He documented their rapid life reviews and feelings of profound peace. This was groundbreaking—one of the first systematic observations of NDE features."

**中文:**
"让我们看看濒死体验研究是如何发展的。1892年，阿尔伯特·海姆与经历事故的登山者进行了开创性工作。他记录了他们的快速人生回顾和深刻的平静感。这是突破性的——这是对濒死体验特征的首批系统观察之一。"

**English:**
"Then, in 1975—more than 80 years later—Raymond Moody published 'Life After Life,' a book based on interviews with over 150 NDErs. Moody proposed what we now call the 'Moody Model' of NDE features. His work brought NDEs into mainstream consciousness. It became a cultural touchstone."

**中文:**
"然后，在1975年——80多年后——雷蒙德·穆迪出版了《生命之后的生命》，这是基于对150多位濒死体验者的访谈。穆迪提出了我们现在称为的NDE特征的"穆迪模型"。他的工作将濒死体验带入主流意识。它成为了文化标志。"

**English:**
"The crucial shift came in 1983, when Bruce Greyson developed the Greyson NDE Scale—a standardized measurement tool. For the first time, researchers could measure NDEs systematically and conduct rigorous empirical studies. This transformed NDE research into genuine science rather than anecdotal documentation."

**中文:**
"关键转变发生在1983年，当时布鲁斯·格雷森开发了格雷森NDE量表——一个标准化测量工具。研究人员第一次能够系统地测量濒死体验并进行严格的经验研究。这将濒死体验研究从轶事记录转变为真正的科学。"

# PART 3: EXISTING KNOWLEDGE GAPS / 第三部分：现有知识空白 (2 minutes)

## Slide: "Existing Knowledge Gaps" / "现有知识空白"

**English:**
"Despite these advances, significant gaps remain. Most NDE research has focused on Western—particularly North American—populations. Research on Asian NDErs is extremely limited."

**中文:**
"尽管取得了这些进展，仍然存在重大空白。大多数濒死体验研究集中在西方——特别是北美——人群。对亚洲濒死体验者的研究极其有限。"

**English:**
"Additionally, many studies rely on retrospective self-reports collected decades after the experience. Few use longitudinal designs to track changes over time. And most importantly, researchers have focused heavily on personality and spiritual changes, but paid less attention to specific outcomes like death anxiety, values transformation, and how cultural frameworks shape the interpretation of NDEs."

**中文:**
"此外，许多研究依赖于几十年后收集的回顾性自我报告。很少有人使用纵向设计来追踪随时间的变化。最重要的是，研究人员主要关注人格和精神变化，但较少关注死亡焦虑、价值观转变等具体结果，以及文化框架如何塑造对濒死体验的解释。"

**English:**
"There's also significant geographic and cultural bias. The phenomenon may manifest differently across cultures, yet we have minimal data on this. In Hong Kong specifically, NDE research has been nearly absent."

**中文:**
"还存在显著的地理和文化偏见。这一现象在不同文化中可能表现不同，但我们对此的数据极少。在香港特别地，濒死体验研究几乎不存在。"

# PART 5: RESEARCH DESIGN & METHODOLOGY / 第五部分：研究设计与方法 (2.5 minutes)

## Slide: "Research Design & Sample" / "研究设计与样本"

**English:**
"We used a mixed-methods approach—combining quantitative measurement with qualitative narrative analysis."

**中文:**
"我们采用了混合方法——结合定量测量和定性叙述分析。"

**English:**
"**For the quantitative portion:** We recruited 30 NDErs from Hong Kong and surrounding regions. These were individuals who'd experienced documented life-threatening events—cardiac arrest, near-drowning, severe trauma, or anesthesia complications. All scored at least 7 on the Greyson NDE Scale, confirming they had genuine NDEs."

**中文:**
"**对于定量部分：** 我们从香港及周边地区招募了30位濒死体验者。这些人都经历过文件记录的危及生命的事件——心脏骤停、溺水、严重创伤或麻醉并发症。所有人的格雷森NDE量表得分至少为7，确认他们有真实的濒死体验。"

**English:**
"Demographics: 53.3% female, 46.7% male. Average age 51.4 years. Religiously diverse—36.7% Buddhist, 26.7% Christian, 20% Taoist, 16.7% secular. Average time since NDE: 8.2 years."

**中文:**
"人口统计：53.3%女性，46.7%男性。平均年龄51.4岁。宗教多样性——36.7%佛教徒，26.7%基督教徒，20%道教徒，16.7%世俗人士。自濒死体验以来的平均时间：8.2年。"

## Slide: "Research Methodology" / "研究方法"

**English:**
"**Quantitative methods:** We used three standardized instruments. The NEO Five-Factor Inventory measured personality traits. The Schwartz Values Survey assessed what participants considered important. The Collett-Lester Fear of Death Scale quantified death anxiety. We compared their scores against published international control data."

**中文:**
"**定量方法：** 我们使用了三个标准化工具。NEO五因素人格清单测量人格特征。施瓦茨价值观调查评估参与者认为重要的东西。科莱特-莱斯特死亡恐惧量表量化死亡焦虑。我们将他们的分数与已发表的国际对照数据进行了比较。"

**English:**
"**Qualitative methods:** We conducted three in-depth semi-structured interviews, each lasting 60-90 minutes, with NDErs representing different religious backgrounds—one Buddhist, one Christian, one secular. We asked detailed questions about their experience, how they changed, how they integrated the experience, and what it meant for their beliefs."

**中文:**
"**定性方法：** 我们进行了三次深入半结构化访谈，每次60-90分钟，与代表不同宗教背景的濒死体验者进行了交谈——一位佛教徒、一位基督教徒、一位世俗人士。我们问了关于他们的经历、他们如何改变、他们如何整合这一经历，以及它对他们信念的意义的详细问题。"

**English:**
"**Innovative features:** Our study included AI-assisted data collection to support participants with limited education or literacy. We ensured socioeconomic diversity—not just wealthy, educated participants. This gave us a more representative sample of Hong Kong's population."

**中文:**
"**创新特点：** 我们的研究包括AI辅助的数据收集，以支持教育程度或识字率有限的参与者。我们确保了社会经济多样性——不仅仅是富裕的、受教育的参与者。这给了我们香港人口更有代表性的样本。"

# PART 6: KEY FINDINGS - PERSONALITY CHANGES / 第六部分：关键发现 - 人格变化 (3 minutes)

## Slide: "Personality Changes Post-NDE" / "濒死体验后的人格变化"

**English:**
"Let's examine our findings, starting with personality. The key finding is stark: NDEs catalyze profound, enduring personality transformations. These effects persist for decades."

**中文:**
"让我们从人格开始检查我们的发现。关键发现很明显：濒死体验催化了深刻的、持久的人格转变。这些效果持续数十年。"

**English:**
"We found three specific changes. First, **heightened altruistic orientation**—increased empathy and compassion. NDErs show significantly elevated scores on measures of empathy compared to control groups. They report being more concerned with others' wellbeing."

**中文:**
"我们发现了三个具体变化。首先，**增强的利他取向**——增加的同情心和怜悯。与对照组相比，濒死体验者在同情心测量上显示出显著升高的分数。他们报告更关心他人的福祉。"

**English:**
"Specifically, Hong Kong NDErs scored 43.8 on Agreeableness, compared to international control data of 38.4. This represents a moderate effect size (Cohen's d = 0.68). But more tellingly, they scored extremely high on emotional sensitivity measures—37.2 compared to control data of 27.4. That's a large effect (d = 1.57)."

**中文:**
"具体来说，香港濒死体验者的宜人性得分为43.8，与国际对照数据的38.4相比。这代表中等效应大小（Cohen's d = 0.68）。但更有说服力的是，他们在情感敏感度测量上得分极高——37.2对比对照数据的27.4。这是一个大效应（d = 1.57）。"

**English:**
"Second, **reduced materialism**. NDErs show significantly lower interest in wealth and material possessions. They report that concerns about status, luxury goods, and financial accumulation—things they cared deeply about before—simply don't matter anymore."

**中文:**
"其次，**减少的物质主义**。濒死体验者对财富和物质财产的兴趣显著降低。他们报告说，对地位、奢侈品和财务积累的关注——他们之前深切关心的事情——根本不再重要了。"

**English:**
"Third, **personality redirected toward greater altruism and spiritual growth**. The overall pattern is unmistakable: people who have NDEs come back fundamentally different. They're kinder, more concerned with meaning than money, more focused on service than success."

**中文:**
"第三，**人格朝向更大的利他主义和精神成长**。总体模式是明确的：经历濒死体验的人回来后本质上是不同的。他们更善良，更关心意义而不是金钱，更关注服务而不是成功。"

# PART 7: KEY FINDINGS - VALUES TRANSFORMATION / 第七部分：关键发现 - 价值观转变 (2.5 minutes)

## Slide: "Values Transformation Post-NDE" / "濒死体验后的价值观转变"

**English:**
"Now let's look at values. The contrast between before and after is striking."

**中文:**
"现在让我们看看价值观。前后的对比很鲜明。"

**English:**
"**Before NDE:** Most participants had external achievement focus—career advancement, wealth accumulation, status symbols. Their worldview was self-centered, even if they weren't aware of it. Success meant climbing the ladder, making money, acquiring things."

**中文:**
"**濒死体验前：** 大多数参与者都有外在成就的焦点——职业晋升、财富积累、地位象征。他们的世界观是以自我为中心的，即使他们没有意识到。成功意味着爬升阶梯、赚钱、获得东西。"

**English:**
"**After NDE:** Their priorities shift dramatically toward meaning-centered goals. Service and altruism become central. They experience what we might call spiritual transcendence—a sense that there's something more important than personal achievement."

**中文:**
"**濒死体验后：** 他们的优先事项朝向以意义为中心的目标大幅转变。服务和利他主义变得核心。他们体验到我们可能称为精神超越的东西——一种感到有比个人成就更重要的东西的感觉。"

**English:**
"The data: Among our 30 participants, before their NDEs, 70% prioritized achievement-oriented goals. After? 76.7% (23 people) now prioritize universal values—concern for all people's wellbeing. 80% (24 people) report significantly reduced interest in wealth. Many made career changes—we have former bankers now working as teachers, counselors, and volunteers."

**中文:**
"数据：在我们的30位参与者中，在濒死体验前，70%优先考虑成就导向的目标。之后？76.7%（23人）现在优先考虑普遍价值观——关心所有人的福祉。80%（24人）报告对财富的兴趣明显减少。许多人改变了职业——我们有曾是银行家现在从事教师、咨询师和志愿者工作的人。"

**English:**
"Evidence comes from international research too. Sutherland (1992) and Greyson & Khanna (2014) documented that these values changes remain stable for decades. This isn't a temporary phase. People don't 'snap back' to their old values. The transformation is lasting."

**中文:**
"证据也来自国际研究。Sutherland（1992）和Greyson & Khanna（2014）记录了这些价值观变化在数十年内保持稳定。这不是暂时阶段。人们不会"恢复"到他们的旧价值观。转变是持久的。"

# PART 8: KEY FINDINGS - SPIRITUAL & RELIGIOUS SHIFTS / 第八部分：关键发现 - 精神与宗教转变 (2.5 minutes)

## Slide: "Religious & Spiritual Belief Shifts" / "宗教与精神信念转变"

**English:**
"Another striking finding concerns spirituality and religion. We observed four key patterns."

**中文:**
"另一个显著发现涉及精神和宗教。我们观察到了四个关键模式。"

**English:**
"**First: Increased belief in afterlife.** Before their NDEs, 46.7% of our participants believed in an afterlife. After? 90% do. A 43.3 percentage-point increase. But here's what's important—it's not just belief change. It's conviction. They don't wonder if there's life after death. They know."

**中文:**
"**首先：增加的来世信念。** 在濒死体验前，我们47.7%的参与者相信来世。之后？90%相信。增加了43.3个百分点。但这里重要的是——这不仅仅是信念改变。这是信念。他们不怀疑死后是否有生命。他们知道。"

**English:**
"**Second: Shift from institutional religion to personal spirituality.** Among our Christian NDErs, 75% increased personal spirituality practices, yet 50% decreased institutional church attendance. They're becoming spiritual without necessarily remaining religious in traditional ways."

**中文:**
"**其次：从制度宗教转向个人精神。** 在我们的基督教濒死体验者中，75%增加了个人精神实践，但50%减少了制度教会参加。他们变得精神，但不一定以传统方式保持宗教性。"

**English:**
"**Third: Enhanced spiritual dependence.** People turn to spiritual frameworks and practices as sources of meaning and resilience. Meditation, prayer, spiritual study—these become central to how they navigate life."

**中文:**
"**第三：增强的精神依赖。** 人们转向精神框架和实践作为意义和韧性的来源。冥想、祈祷、精神研究——这些成为他们如何驾驭生活的核心。"

**English:**
"**Fourth: Cultural variation.** This is crucial for our research. The expression of spiritual transformation differs across cultures. Our Buddhist NDErs integrated their experiences through Buddhist cosmology—they said, 'This is exactly what the Tibetan Book of the Dead describes.' Our Christian NDErs interpreted through divine encounter. Our secular NDErs developed personal philosophies about consciousness."

**中文:**
"**第四：文化变异。** 这对我们的研究至关重要。精神转变的表达在不同文化中是不同的。我们的佛教濒死体验者通过佛教宇宙论整合他们的经历——他们说'这正是《西藏死亡之书》描述的'。我们的基督教濒死体验者通过神圣相遇进行了解释。我们的世俗濒死体验者发展了关于意识的个人哲学。"

# PART 9: KEY FINDINGS - DEATH ANXIETY REDUCTION / 第九部分：关键发现 - 死亡焦虑减少 (2 minutes)

## Slide: "Reduction in Death Anxiety" / "死亡焦虑的减少"

**English:**
"Perhaps the most significant and consistent finding in all NDE research is reduction in death anxiety. Our data strongly confirms this."

**中文:**
"也许所有濒死体验研究中最显著和一致的发现是死亡焦虑的减少。我们的数据强烈确认了这一点。"

**English:**
"Using the Collett-Lester Fear of Death Scale, our NDErs averaged 42.3, compared to international control groups averaging 79.4. That's a difference of 37.1 points—representing an effect size of d = -2.01, which is considered very large. In practical terms, it means NDErs report dramatically lower death anxiety."

**中文:**
"使用科莱特-莱斯特死亡恐惧量表，我们的濒死体验者平均为42.3，而国际对照组平均为79.4。这是37.1分的差异——代表d = -2.01的效应大小，被认为是非常大的。从实际来说，这意味着濒死体验者报告的死亡焦虑明显降低。"

**English:**
"The key mechanism appears to be **altered meaning-making**. Most NDErs report dramatic decreases in fear of death. Enhanced life appreciation follows—death becomes less threatening; life becomes more meaningful. And yes, some temporary distress or confusion is reported initially, but the overall trajectory is reduced existential fear."

**中文:**
"关键机制似乎是**改变的意义构建**。大多数濒死体验者报告对死亡的恐惧大幅下降。随之而来的是增强的生活欣赏——死亡变得不那么威胁；生活变得更有意义。是的，最初报告了一些暂时的痛苦或困惑，但总体轨迹是减少了存在焦虑。"

**English:**
"Specifically, 93.3% of our participants reported death fear nearly completely eliminated. One NDEr told us: 'Death used to be my greatest fear. After experiencing it, I'm completely at peace. I know death is just a transition. There's nothing to fear.'"

**中文:**
"具体来说，93.3%的参与者报告死亡恐惧几乎完全消除。一位濒死体验者告诉我们：'死亡曾是我最大的恐惧。经历它后，我完全平和。我知道死亡只是一个转变。没有什么可怕的。'"

# PART 11: QUALITATIVE THEMES / 第十一部分：定性主题 (3 minutes)

## Slide: "Key Research Findings" / "关键研究发现"

**中文:**
